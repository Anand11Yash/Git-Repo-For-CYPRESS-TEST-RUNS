Carbon Footprint Calculator

The lack of flexibility in the existing Carbon Footprint Calculators for modifying criteria, adding and removing specific activities led to the creation of this Calculator, where we welcome all to contribute to this project by making positive modifications for improving its accuracy or for personal use.

Understanding Carbon Footprint
Carbon Footprint is the total amount of emissions of Greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, hydrochlorofluorocarbons and some other gases, into the environment by our activities or natural activities.

A Carbon Footprint Calculator on the other hand, is a tool which is used to calculate the Carbon Footprint contributed to the environment. Different Calculators give different results based on location.

For instance, a calculator designed for Americans will not give the same result for Indians, the reason being that there is a difference in Carbon Factors of different activities in different nations.

For the calculation of Carbon Footprint, it is important to know the consumption of an activity and what its respective Carbon Factor is. Carbon Factors are used as a standard to calculate the emission of various Greenhouse gases per unit of different activities.
